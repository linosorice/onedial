01 / Tonal Zones

From glass to fire in a single sweep

Five neural amp zones that morph seamlessly as you turn. No menu diving. No preset surfing. No decision fatigue. Just turn the knob and play.

Clean
0 — 2
Fender-style glass. Every chord rings with bell-like clarity — perfect for fingerpicking, jazz voicings, and ambient swells.
Warm
2 — 4
That sweet spot where tubes start to breathe. Roll back your volume for clean, dig in for grit. The tone your blues heroes live in.
Crunch
4 — 6
Classic rock backbone. The sound of cranked Marshalls and Saturday night gigs. Touch-sensitive and alive under your fingers.
Drive
6 — 8
Tight, aggressive, modern. Palm mutes that punch, power chords that roar. Built for drop tunings and heavy riffing.
Lead
8 — 10
Wide-open, endless sustain. Notes bloom into feedback, pinch harmonics leap out. This is where solos write themselves.
